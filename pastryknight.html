Pastry Knight

As part of my studies, I signed up for a Game Development minor for 6 months and learned the core of making a video game.
With a group of friends we set up a team, each member followed their prefered tracks (Storytelling, Programming, Concept Art, Artificial Intelligence, 3D & Animation...) and we leared to work together to build a game.

Pastry Knight is a roguelike / dungeon crawler reminiscent adventure that follows the brave Pastry Knight on his tumultuous path to rescuing his daughter.
