My name is Morgan Gibrila, I am a UI / UX Designer and I am discovering who I am as a designer. I love challenging myself, whether it is in my designs or in my hobbies.

Over the course of the last 3 years I have being learning what it is to be a designer, and have applied it on several of my projects, from: designing and coding the HUD / Menu of a video game, researching solutions to encourage people to engage with their community, to coding this portfolio myself.

I want to explore the world of design by working with other designers on a range of projects, keep challenging myself to create new, outside the box, design, and learn new skills.
